Smart Farming Tips

Practical advice to improve productivity and sustainability.

💧

Irrigation

Water crops early morning or evening to reduce evaporation. Use drip irrigation to save water.

🌱

Soil Health

Add compost or organic manure regularly. Test your soil once a year for nutrients.

🐛

Pest Control

Rotate crops to reduce pests. Try neem oil spray before chemical pesticides.

⚡

Smart Practices

Choose disease-resistant crop varieties and check weather before sowing or harvesting.

🧪

Fertilizers

Apply fertilizers in smaller split doses. Avoid overuse to protect long-term soil health.
